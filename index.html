ACTIVITIES

1. Snorkeling at Maafushi Island: The underwater Coral Gardens of Maafushi Island is home to some of the most colourful marine life, such as Manta rays, Moray eels, Green Sea Turtles, sturgeons, and other exotic fish.
2. Go for Jet Ski at Male Atoll: Jet skiing is often rated as one of the best things to do in Maldives by a majority of the travellers who have visited this island paradise. The uninterrupted water surface of the ocean at Male Atoll deems the place as one of the safest spots to learn and do jet skiing.
3. Parasailing: Cruising across the sky and feeling the cool sea breeze kiss the face is one of the most exciting things to do in Maldives, which can be best experienced by trying parasailing. While you enjoy parasailing over the azure waters of the ocean, you can soak in the spectacular panoramic views of the entire archipelago.





SPOTS

Maldives Glowing Beach

The Maldives Glowing Beach is situated at the Mudhdhoo Island, also known as the Vaadhoo Island in Raa Atoll. Beach is one of the most visited tourist destinations in these islands, mostly known for its phenomenal fluorescent, glowing waves. This natural phenomenon occurs due to the presence of millions of marine microbes known as phytoplankton in the water. This phytoplankton is bioluminescent and as the skies get darker after sunset, these microscopic planktons emit a bright vibrant blue light in the water which creates an ethereal effect. This phenomenon makes the waves glow as they gently splash over the beach and is the ideal place to spend your time after sundown in the Maldives.

Vaadhoo Island

Vaadhoo Island is a beautiful island in the Maldives which offers beautiful beaches, iconic landscapes and mesmerising beauty. When the sun goes down at Vaadhoo Island, the sea waves lapping the shore light up like a shimmering field of blue dots giving the sea the appearance of the starlit night sky.

Gulhi Island

Gulhi is a charming island in the Kaafu Atoll, Maldives, known for its stunning beaches and laid-back atmosphere. Gulhi Island: Explore the beauty of Gulhi Island, with its crystal-clear turquoise waters and white sandy beaches.
